### Notes

V1 of the [Insights for Android mobile app](/docs/mobile-apps/insights-app/features/insights-mobile-app) was released today. It includes support for viewing dashboards and individual widgets. The app is designed to run on both mobile phones and tablets.

### Features

* View all your favorite dashboards and widgets
* Drill down and get a detailed view of widgets
* Share your widget detail with others
* Multi-user and multi-account support
